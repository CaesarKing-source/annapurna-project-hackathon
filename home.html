# Welcome to Annapurna?


- Annapurna, a platform where you can help the needy person by donating and contribute towards hunger problems' solution in India.

 

Features..

1. Heat Maps


We will be using google maps to plot all the nearby food banks where donor can donate food. Donor can also find those food banks through this feature with directions to easily reach there.

2. Web Games


We will introduce mini games with google ads so that we can earn money through ads and give a part of it to the games player which will result in mutual benefit making this app beneficial. In this way user can earn coins and we can also collect money and use it in well being of needy.

3. Fund Sharing


If there is no wasted food in one's house but he/she is willing to contribute in it then they can donate money through this feature. This will help the food banks to use transportation to reach the destination faster or other means of helping needy.
